Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Baby Kick Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection

Baby Kick Tracker is designed with privacy in mind. We want you to know:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.
  • Local Storage Only: All data you enter in the app (kick logs, session data, baby name) is stored locally on your device.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.
  • No Accounts: The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Kick timestamps and counts
  • Meal session data
  • Baby name (if you choose to add one)

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Apple Watch Sync

If you use Baby Kick Tracker on Apple Watch, data is synced between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This sync happens directly between your devices and does not involve any external servers.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

  • Device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
  • iOS encryption
  • App sandboxing

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Using your device's Settings to clear app data

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by expecting parents and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
